Summary

The Queen’s Innovation Connector Summer Initiative (QICSI) is an intensive 17-week program offering young innovators a stipend and seed funding to support the launch of their ventures. QICSI provides the opportunity to work part-time while receiving ongoing support from the Dunin-Deshpande Queen's Innovation Centre advisors, mentors, and staff. This year's stream focuses on creating a solution that addresses the various challenges created by Covid-19.

Since my original summer plan failed, I started the idea of data analysis with Covid-19. After that, I found several friends with good academic records and we built this team together. Our team used our part time to finish this project. This summer, we were keeping learning and exploring opportunities. At first, we defined our problem as risk factors of diseases, possible end-users as public, governments and public healths, and we will create a website to let people be aware of the harmful disease. We did some interviews and questionnaires toward the public, and received good feedback. However, governments and public healths rejected our collaborations, since they have other companies as their partners. I understand this since we are just students with lack of experience and skills. Also, our mentors told us, this way for the project is a research project, not solving a real-life problem. By the guide of a mentor, we tried to do the disease contact tracing map that almost starts from zero. I know this is a good way to solve a real-life problem, but it is hard to compete with the government's contact tracing system. They can directly contact the patient, and locate their movement history. At this time, our project has almost failed, but I thought that we must make something for the Covid-19. So now we are still doing the data analysis rather than the contact tracing map. Since there is no organization that can collaborate with us, we need to spread our solutions by ourselves, to let more people know more about Covid-19. In summary, though there are some failures, we successfully made the analysis and website for the Covid-19.

Hopefully you can help us to spread our solutions.

Tao Shan Covid-19 Data analysis & Web Developing Queen’s Innovation Connector Summer Initiative (QICSI)
